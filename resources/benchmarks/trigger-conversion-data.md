## The Multi-Signal Lift Multipliers

Signals do not exist in a vacuum. Applying multiple signals to a single account exponentially increases the likelihood of conversion.

- **Baseline (1 Signal):** E.g., Just targeting a new VP of Sales. (5% Reply Rate).
- **Stack 2 Signals:** E.g., New VP of Sales + Recent Series B. (Approx. +50% relative lift).
- **Stack 3 Signals:** E.g., New VP of Sales + Recent Series B + Installed Outreach.io. (Approx. +120% relative lift).

*The Golden Rule of List Building:* A list of 50 accounts showing 3 stacked signals will outperform a list of 1,000 accounts showing 1 widespread signal.
