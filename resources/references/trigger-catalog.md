# Trigger Catalog and Taxonomy

A categorized reference of over 30 identifiable GTM triggers. Use this to determine what to look for based on user constraints.

## 1. Top-Tier "Bridgebound" Triggers (Highest Conversion)
*Context:* Prior relationship exists.
1. **Past Customer to New ICP Account:** Power user moved to a target account.
2. **Current Customer to New ICP Account:** Current user leaves, you must pitch their new company and defend the old one.
3. **Closed Lost Revisit:** A deal lost to "timing/budget" hitting the 6-month or 12-month mark.
4. **Investor Portfolio Intro:** Target account is backed by the same PE/VC firm as your company.

## 2. Executive Personnel Triggers (New Mandates)
*Context:* New leaders bring new budgets and their own playbooks.
1. **New C-Level Executive:** (e.g., New CMO means new marketing strategy/tools).
2. **New VP/Director:** (The operational leaders executing the new strategy).
3. **Massive Hiring Spree (Specific Dept):** e.g., Hiring 20+ SDRs indicates aggressive outbound goals (and pain around training/ramp).
4. **Layoffs (Specific Dept):** e.g., Laying off 20 SDRs means they need to automate more with fewer people. (Handle with extreme empathy).

## 3. Firmographic & Financial Triggers (New Money)
*Context:* High budget availability.
1. **Funding Round (Series A - D):** Immediate pressure to deploy cash for growth.
2. **Mergers & Acquisitions:** Massive pain regarding integrating tech stacks and data.
3. **IPO Announcement:** Focus shifts to compliance, security, and GAAP reporting.
4. **New Fiscal Year/Quarter:** Budget refresh (Q1) or Use-It-Or-Lose-It (Q4).

## 4. Technographic Triggers (The Stack)
*Context:* Identifiable via scraping tools (BuiltWith).
1. **Competitor Install:** They just chose the enemy. (Wait 6-11 months to prey on renewal frustration).
2. **Complementary Install:** They just bought a tool that integrates perfectly with yours.
3. **Contract Renewal Approaching:** You know they sign annual deals, they installed 10 months ago.

## 5. Inbound/Intent Triggers (The "I'm Looking" Signals)
*Context:* The buyer is active right now.
1. **Pricing Page Deanonymization:** Clearbit/RB2B identifies an ICP visitor on the /pricing URL.
2. **High-Value Content Download:** They gave their email for a highly specific technical report.
3. **G2/TrustRadius Category Surge:** The company is reading competitor reviews.
4. **Webinar/Event Attendance:** They spent 45 minutes listening to your founder speak.
